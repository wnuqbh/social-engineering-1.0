Timetable Dropped for Semester 2, 2025/2026

Dear Students,

Please be informed that if your timetable for the current semester has been dropped, it is due to one or more of the following reasons:

  1. Outstanding fees / incomplete payment from previous semester.
  2. Outstanding fees / incomplete payment of proforma invoice (PIXXXX).
  3. Failure to comply with academic requirements (GPA below 2.00 for PTPTN sponsor).
  4. Sponsorship status was not updated or confirmed for the current semester.
  5. Other related compliance issues.

To avoid further inconvenience, you are advised to take immediate action by:

  • Reviewing your student's ledger and make necessary payment. If you have already made the payment, please contact the Finance Helpdesk at financehelpdesk@uniten.edu.my. Once verified, we will escalate the case to the Academic Registrar's Office to re-register your timetable.
  • Liaising with the relevant department (Finance) for clarification and necessary rectification.

Please be reminded that failure to resolve this matter within the stipulated period may affect your academic progress for the semester. Should you require further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact the relevant office.

Thank you for your attention and cooperation.

You have outstanding debt and are not allowed to view your online tickets or examination results. For further information, please contact the Finance Helpdesk.
